This is from the great movie called Fiddler on the Roof from 1971. Here, it tells a brief story of the wonderful relationship between the eldest daughter Tzeitel and the village tailor whose name is Motel.

As "master of the house", one responsibility is to see that his first three daughters are married off when age appropriate. So this story can begin with the village matchmaker Yente. Tevye, the poor milkman of the village, who is Tzeitel's father, agrees to this match with Lazar Wolf here as it will ensure his daughter's future as prosperous.
